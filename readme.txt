== Installation ==

1. Go to Plugins in your WordPress administration, click Add New, and search for “webcake”.
2. Now click at Install Now button.
3. Click on Activate after upload has completed.
4. Go to Settings and choose Webcake.
5. Log into your Webcake account.
6. Publishing your landing page.
